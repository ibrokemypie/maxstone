MaxStone


Combine the MaxStone Wireless Camera Remote with a compatible iPhone, iPod, or iPad to control your camera's shutter.

Able to work with many popular cameras, the MaxStone is a versatile trigger.

It attaches to your camera's strap loop so it can hang over the infrared sensor.


MaxStone Wireless Camera Remote

for DSLR Cameras

Using Bluetooth 4.0 and infrared technology, the MaxStone connects your device to your camera, allowing you to remotely control the camera's shutter from up to 82' using the MaxStone iOS App. The app also allows you to track your camera, set a time-lapse, video, or bulb setting. A button on the MaxStone itself can trigger your iPhone from up to 16' away, and you can set a delay to 3, 5, 7, or 10 seconds, giving you plenty of time to prepare your selfie.
